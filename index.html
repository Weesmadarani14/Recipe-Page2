Best Tasting Ribeye Recipe

Ribeye steaks are some of the most tender steaks in the world. This simple recipe will make you steaks that will have your family and friends asking for more.

Preparation time

  • Total Time: 30 minutes
  • Prep Time: 5 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 15 minutes
  • Rest Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 ribeye steaks
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter divided
  • 2 cloves minced garlic
  • Chopped fresh rosemary optional

Instructions

  1. Bring the steaks to room temperature 30 minutes prior to cooking.
  2. Heat your cast iron skillet to medium-high heat.
  3. Season the steaks with the kosher salt and ground black pepper.
  4. When is skillet is hot, add the olive oil. Then, place the steaks on the pan.
  5. Cook the steaks for about 3-4 minutes to create the initial sear.
  6. Flip the steaks and cook for about 2-3 minutes or more depending on how you like your steaks cooked. If you're having trouble figuring out if your steak is cooked to your desired level, use a thermometer.
  7. Top each steak with butter, garlic, and rosemary. Optional
